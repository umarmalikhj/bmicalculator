# bmicalculator

A simple BMI calculator built with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.  
It takes height (in feet) and weight (in kg), calculates the BMI, and shows the health category.

---

## How It Works

1. Enter your height (in feet) and weight (in kilograms).
2. Click **Calculate**.
3. The app converts height to meters and shows your BMI with a category message.

---

## Formula

(1 foot = 0.3048 meters)

---

## Categories

| Category | BMI Range |
|-----------|------------|
| Underweight | ≤ 18.5 |
| Normal | 18.5 – 24.9 |
| Overweight | 25 – 29.9 |
| Obese I | 30 – 34.9 |
| Obese II | 35 – 39.9 |
| Morbidly Obese | ≥ 40 |
